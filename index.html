FAQ

Can I record messgages?
Yes, kids love to get videos and even letters. This app lets you record them and leave them on the table to be disovered by your children later on.
Can we play games together -- don't other sites do this?
Yes, other sites do do this, but our site does it better because it's combined with all the other things you'd find on the table: scissors, drawing pads, music, puzzles, homework, reward charts etc.
